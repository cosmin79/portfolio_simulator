### Notes
- All tickers use `auto_adjust=True` (dividends reinvested) which matches the simulator's design.
- Commodities (GSG) is the main data bottleneck for many portfolios — it only goes back to 2006-07.
- BIL (T-Bills) starts 2007-05, limiting several portfolios that include cash positions.
- DLS is used for "International Small Cap Value" since AVDV only goes back to 2019.
- SCZ is used for "International Small Cap Blend" since VSS only goes back to 2009.
- The site's "Developed Large Cap" in the Global Market Portfolio is mapped to VTI since US equities dominate the developed market cap.
